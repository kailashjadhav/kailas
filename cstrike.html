About C-Strike

Get IN Position and Wait for my call….

This event is for enthusiasm of participants. Helping Aavishkar in its Financial problems
Presented by Department of Information Technology
Day One & Event
On 18th & 19th Feb 2017
It's a Non-Technical Event
Max 4 person can participate in this event
Registration fee is Rs.50/- per participant
Rounds
Phase I(Knockout Round)
Map - Inferno, 1st Round-Knife (Toss Round) total 11 rounds, switch after 5 rounds. Qualifying team will move to next Phase.
Phase II
Map - Dust 2 1st Round-Knife (Toss Round) total 19 rounds, switch after 9 rounds. Qualifying teams will move to Final Phase.
Phase III
Phase 3 (Final Round): Map – Train 1st Round-Knife (Toss Round) total 23 rounds, switch after 11 rounds.
Note:
1st day Final winners can compete with 2nd day final winner for achieving double prize money.
Rules

1) Game once Started cannot be interrupted.
2) No use of Bullpup and Creg.
3) No cheats allowed except consoling.
4) No scroll-duck allowed.
5) External mouse, Headphones and keyboards are allowed.
6) Participants must not mishandle the computer system. Any damage caused, will lead to direct disqualification and the cost of the damage if any will be paid by the participants.
7) Use of offensive language by any participant will lead to team disqualification.
