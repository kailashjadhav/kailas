About Kalaavishkar

Painting Competition
Kalaavishkar is an event which is going to be organize under the 'AAVISHKAR'. This event is continued after watching the enthusiasm of the participants as well as the team member of the group. This event provides platform to the interested students in painting and drawing. It also helps students in developing drawing skill which will help in their professional life. Basically, this event is related to drawing and painting activity. This event gives you a task to draw or paint that you have to complete in a given time. Kalaavishkar is ready to show the painter inside you & turn your hobby into reward & creativity into appreciation.
But are you READY ?
Two Day Event
On 18th & 19th Feb 2016
It's a Non-Technical Event
A single person can participate
Registration fee is Rs. 100/-
Rules
1. Artist has to prepare the painting in 3 hrs. at the place of the event.
2. Any one from following categories of painting are permitted.
Portrait
Landscape
Spot Painting
3. There will be separate entries.
4. Any type of medium is allowed (sketching, charcoal, water-color, oil-pastels or combination of all).
5. Bring your own colors.
6. We will provide any one drawing sheet from the following sizes A2 / A3
