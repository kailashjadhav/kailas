Cyber Security Club

The term “Cyber Security” refers to the state of being protected against the criminal or unauthorized use of electronic data, or the measures taken to achieve this. With increasing use of online transaction & Digital India, the need of online security is essential. Security here refers to maintaining access to data & resources, Identifying threats, protecting from various dangers factors & attacks, ..., etc.

Objective:

Our focus is on making people digitally literate and at the same time securing them in this digital world.

  1. Making people digitally literate, promoting the use of online services to help Digital India.
  2. Providing knowledge to protect security violence.
  3. Protecting from various attacks & viruses.
  4. Protecting from phishing attacks.
  5. Spreading awareness on the use of various security features.
  6. Conduct seminar and workshop on the various aspect of cyber security.

The cyber security club at GCE Karad is a student’s club not for profit organization which is dedicated to secure everyone is in the digital world, along with that we will also take care of our college security issues & college level forensic issues.
