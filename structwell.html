Details
1. Make simply supported beam of size 65*65*500 mm.
2. Using pop sticks & fevicol, without using any strengthening coating of paint or fevicol.
3. Design beam for max 35kg & min. 15kg load.
4. Beam is to be loaded gradually at center.
5. Tolerance : -5 mm to +5 mm.
6. Formula:- points = Max load taken/Self wt. of beam.
7. Max deflection of beam at center is 1cm.
