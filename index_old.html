About aavishkar

Those who want to achieve something, never point a finger at anybody, take the burden on their shoulder and carry it.
Aavishkar is a National Level Technical Symposium.

This event is conducted with great compassion and passion by GCEKians it is platform that provide the students a great place to show off their technical and professional knowledge and skills.
Aavishkar 2k17 is a event that put test to the wid, ability and talent of every individual who participate in it. Avishkar 2k17 is not a chance its an opportunity for every aspirer to get exposed to scrupulously conceded ideas loathed with technical knowledge.
Every year every GCEKian spents every last moment of his very life to produce an event which we call with gratitude Aavishkar for every tech lover to be part of an enjoy, which is made with every drop of over seat an every inch of our honour.
